Financial Statements Explained Simply w/ Brian Feroldi
======================================================
URL:  https://www.theinvestorspodcast.com/episodes/financial-statements-explained-simply-w-brian-feroldi/
Date: 11 September 2025

On today’s episode, Clay brings back long-time guest, Brian Feroldi, to educate our listeners on how to analyze financial statements. 
Brian Feroldi is the founder of Long-Term Mindset, which creates educational content that can help anyone to learn how the stock market works. His newsletter has over 100,000 readers, and he is one of the best at making investing as easy and simple as possible.

SUBSCRIBE

IN THIS EPISODE, YOU’LL LEARN:
An overview of the three financial statements.
What GAAP accounting is and why the US is a good environment for investors from a regulatory perspective.
Why Brian prefers that companies pay employees in cash rather than through stock based compensation.
Why each dollar should be treated differently when analyzing different businesses.
The role of financial statements in estimating a company’s intrinsic value.
Why the P/E ratio is a useless valuation metric for growth businesses.
Why Brian prioritizes optionality in his investment process.
Red flags to look out for in the financial statements.
What David Gardner taught Brian about investing.
Why good investing is all about marrying the right side of your brain with the left side of your brain.
And so much more!
