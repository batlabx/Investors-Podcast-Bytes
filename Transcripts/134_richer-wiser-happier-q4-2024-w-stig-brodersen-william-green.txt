On today’s show, Stig Brodersen talks with co-host William Green, the author of “Richer, Wiser, Happier.” With a strong focus on building meaningful relationships and traveling with friends along the path, they discuss what has made them Richer, Wiser, or Happier in the past quarter

IN THIS EPISODE, YOU’LL LEARN:
Why the opposite of a virtue is also a virtue.
Why the Nomad Partnership principles resonate so deeply with the value investing community.
Why does the value investing community have some similarities to a cult, and whether it is a problem.
Why you need to hold two contradictory thoughts in your head and still stay sane.
How to travel with friends along the path in business and life.
How to think about partnerships and relationships with the titans in the investing industry.
Where to find the intersection between authenticity and honesty.
Why it simplifies your life when you put quality front and center.
