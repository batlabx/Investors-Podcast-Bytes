In today’s episode of The Investor’s Podcast, formerly known as We Study Billionaires, Kyle Grieve is joined by co-host Daniel Mahncke to discuss Wise PLC, a London-listed FinTech company that enables cheap and fast cross-border payments by matching local flows rather than moving money internationally through costly SWIFT networks. They’ll explore Wise’s founding story, its four revenue streams (cross-border payments, card revenue, customer deposit interest, and Wise Platform), and the powerful flywheel that creates economies of scale shared with customers through continuously declining take rates and decreased transaction times. The episode also covers Wise’s competitive moat against banks, remittance companies, and other FinTechs, as well as Kyle’s valuation framework.
