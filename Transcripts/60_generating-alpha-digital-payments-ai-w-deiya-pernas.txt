========================================================

On today’s episode, Clay is joined by Deiya Pernas to discuss his investing philosophy, why he sees opportunity for investors in the digital payments space, and why he is closely following developments in AI.
After a decade of experience in the investment industry, Deiya co-founded Pernas Research with his brother in 2023. Since January 2017, the Pernas portfolio has published audited net returns of 27.1% versus the S&P 500’s average annualized return of 14.7%.
