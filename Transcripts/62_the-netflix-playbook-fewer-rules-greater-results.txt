The Netflix Playbook: Fewer Rules, Greater Results
==================================================
URL:  https://www.theinvestorspodcast.com/episodes/the-netflix-playbook-fewer-rules-greater-results/
Date: 28 August 2025

On today’s episode, Kyle Grieve discusses the radical culture that helped Netflix dominate Blockbuster and achieve massive long-term success. He explores lessons on talent density, candor, feedback, freedom, and responsibility that reveal how culture can become a company’s greatest competitive moat.

SUBSCRIBE

IN THIS EPISODE, YOU’LL LEARN:
Why fewer rules can unlock a company’s peak performance.
The cultural failures that doomed Blockbuster against Netflix.
How talent density drives innovation and rapid productivity gains.
Why subtraction sometimes creates stronger, higher-performing teams.
How candor accelerates decision-making and organizational efficiency.
Netflix’s 4A framework for delivering and receiving feedback.
Why “rockstar pay” beats incentive bonuses for top talent.
How sunshining mistakes builds trust and stronger organizations.
The Keeper Test for retaining only your best performers.
Why leading with context—not control—fuels lasting innovation.
And so much more!
