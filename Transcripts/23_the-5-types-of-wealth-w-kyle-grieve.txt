On today’s episode, Kyle Grieve discusses a refreshingly different take on wealth inspired by The Five Types of Wealth by Sahil Bloom. Rather than focusing solely on money, the conversation explores a more holistic framework that includes time, relationships, health, purpose, and financial independence. Kyle shares personal reflections and practical ideas you can use to build a richer, more fulfilling life.

IN THIS EPISODE, YOU’LL LEARN:
Why you should reframe wealth beyond asset accumulation
The five types of wealth and how they shape a fulfilling life
How wealth is built during life, not after reaching a destination
How defining personal identity clarifies long-term decisions
Why goals and anti-goals protect success and relationships
How time wealth depends on awareness, attention, and control
Why relationships are the strongest predictor of happiness and health
How curiosity and purpose can drive mental wealth
Why physical wealth is easy to overlook
How defining enough creates financial wealth
