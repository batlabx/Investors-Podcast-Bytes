On today’s episode, Kyle Grieve discusses the remarkable rise of Fairfax Financial and its founder-led CEO, Prem Watsa. He’ll explore how disciplined value investing and insurance float fueled decades of compounding, and created a resilient business that withstood short sellers, the tech bubble, and the GFC. Kyle will also share key lessons on capital allocation, culture, and business legacy.
