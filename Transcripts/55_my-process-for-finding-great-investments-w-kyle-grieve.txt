My Process for Finding Great Investments w/ Kyle Grieve
=======================================================
URL:  https://www.theinvestorspodcast.com/episodes/my-process-for-finding-great-investments-w-kyle-grieve/
Date: 20 September 2025

On today’s episode, Kyle Grieve discusses the lessons that shaped his investing philosophy, from painful early mistakes in crypto to building a disciplined framework focused on quality businesses and high-potential, undervalued microcaps. He shares how goals, temperament, and a business-owner mindset guide his decisions, and why compounding, capital efficiency, and management integrity are the cornerstones of his strategy.

SUBSCRIBE

IN THIS EPISODE, YOU’LL LEARN:
Lessons learned from the early days as a pure speculator.
Kyle’s simple investing goal and the pros and cons of pursuing it.
Why benchmarks are irrelevant to his approach.
How adopting a business-owner mindset helps compound capital.
The two categories of investments that form the foundation of his investing strategy.
Why quality lies on a spectrum and why the highest quality business isn’t always the best investment choice.
The importance of skilled and aligned management.
The three main reasons Kyle sells a stock.
How to combat confirmation bias and the sunk cost fallacy.\
Why averaging up has been more effective than averaging down.
And so much more!
