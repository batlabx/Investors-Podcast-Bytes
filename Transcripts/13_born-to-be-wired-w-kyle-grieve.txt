In today’s episode, Kyle Grieve discusses the life and career of legendary capital allocator John Malone and details the at times complex strategies that helped him compound capital over decades. He explores Malone’s mastery of leverage, tax-efficient deal structuring, decentralized leadership, and asymmetric risk taking, while also reflecting on the challenges of complexity, disruption, and succession. Throughout the episode, Kyle distills Malone’s lifeboat framework for survival and long-term compounding, emphasizing downside protection, intelligent optionality, and the discipline to think independently in a world driven by bidding wars and short-term thinking.
